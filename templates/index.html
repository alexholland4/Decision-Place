Make decisions faster and more confidently!

Overwhelmed by too many options? The "Paradox of Choice" suggests that while we might believe more choices are better, having too many can actually lead to decision paralysis and dissatisfaction. This tool helps you overcome this by breaking down complex decisions into simple, pairwise comparisons.

By choosing between two items at a time, you intuitively build a ranked list of your preferences, reducing cognitive load and leading to more confident decisions.

Inspired by research on behavior science and decision-making. Learn more at The Decision Lab.
